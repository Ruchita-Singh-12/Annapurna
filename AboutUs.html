About Us

Annapurna is a web application designed to provide homemade tasty and healthy food to hostellers in collaboration with housewives. The main objective behind designing this application is women empowerment as the housewives can use their cooking skills to earn money. Apart from that the hostellers get the facility to avail homemade food.The delivery is fast and the payment can be done both online as well as offline.So,Hurrry up and register to our website to avail the facilities.
